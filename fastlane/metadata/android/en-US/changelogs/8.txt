PixelPlayerOSS 0.7.0-foss:

* Prepared the OSS edition for public source builds with release, privacy, and security documentation.
* Removed non-OSS-oriented integrations and Google Play Services runtime dependencies.
* Kept optional online lookups user-controlled: Navidrome/Subsonic, Jellyfin, LRCLIB, and Deezer artwork.
* Added smart playlist creation from local listening history and favorites.
* Improved release builds so missing local signing keys produce unsigned artifacts instead of dummy-signed APKs.
