PixelPlayerOSS 0.7.0-foss:

* Initial FOSS release tracking the PixelPlayer 0.7.0 line.
* Removed non-FOSS and Google Play oriented integrations: Telegram, NetEase, QQ Music, Google Drive, Gemini, Cast, Wear OS, Play Store billing, Firebase, Crashlytics, Google Play Services runtime dependencies, and public Last.fm/ListenBrainz scrobbling.
* Kept local playback, Navidrome/Subsonic, Jellyfin, LRCLIB lyrics, and Deezer artist artwork as user-controlled features.
* Added F-Droid metadata, license documentation, unsigned release build support, and security hardening for cloud streaming and backup restore.
